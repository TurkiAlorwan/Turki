The Brown Bear

INTRODUCTION:

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is native to parts of northern Eurasia and North America_ Its conservation status is currently "Least Concern." There are many subspecies within the brown bear species. including the Atlas bear and the Himalayan brown bear.
